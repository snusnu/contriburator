Substance > Collaborative authoring in realtime

When you look at usual online media, you'll realize that there's a lot of noise around. When you're reading stories online, you encounter blinking text, flashy ads and such, which most users might find disturbing. We believe it doesn't have to be this way.

The core idea of Substance is to strictly separate content from representation and to reduce complexity (in terms of visual clutter) to a minimum. Therefore the editor only allows for semantic annotation of text- fragments. Thus, writers don't choose the font, size and color of text, they instead annotate the semantical meaning (Section, Text, Quotation, etc.). Document rendering takes place in a separate second step. We tried to make renderings pretty by default. In future you may be able to customize them. Substance docu- ments are composed of Content Nodes, which are arranged hierarchically. It's on purpose that hierarchy is not hidden from the user. However the docs are written sequentially, as usual. We abandoned the usage of a markup language in favor of a Semantic Rich Text Editor.
